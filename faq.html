FAQ

Endeleza Initiative is a non-profit organization founded by a group of models who believe in using fashion and modeling to create positive social change.

What does "Endeleza" mean?

"Endeleza" is a Swahili word that means "to move forward" or "to progress."

Who is Endeleza Initiative's target population?

Our target population can vary depending on the specific program or activity. We may focus on youth, women, underprivileged communities, or others depending on the initiative.

How does Endeleza Initiative use fashion and modeling for social good?

We use our expertise in these areas to:

* Organize charity fashion shows.

* Hold design competitions focused on social awareness or sustainability.

* Advocate for ethical and sustainable practices in the fashion industry..
