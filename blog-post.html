NATIONAL TREE PLANTING

With conjunction to the ministry of Environment Models also took part in Tree planting for a green country

Trees planted by models and fashion designers hold significance beyond beautification. These initiatives raise awareness about environmental issues like deforestation and climate change, especially considering the fashion industry's environmental footprint. By planting trees, these figures can inspire fans and the industry as a whole to embrace sustainability. The act itself symbolizes growth, renewal, and a healthier planet, reflecting a commitment to a more eco-conscious future. Furthermore, planting trees can be a way for models and designers to connect with their communities and participate in local environmental efforts..
